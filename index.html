About

Laura Mendija Rozīte

Visual & Multidisciplinary Designer

I design and build digital products, interfaces, and campaign systems, working across UI/UX, front-end, motion, and visual communication.

My work focuses on turning complex ideas and products into clear, structured experiences – from responsive websites in ecommerce platforms to large-scale campaign systems spanning digital, print, and physical retail. I regularly move between design and implementation, translating concepts directly into working front-end solutions.

I graduated in Visual Communication from the Latvian Academy of Art, where I developed a strong foundation across typography, composition, image, motion, and spatial design. Since graduating, I’ve applied those skills in commercial environments, leading real-world projects end-to-end – from UX logic and visual systems to final execution and launch.

I’m comfortable owning projects end-to-end – from concept and UX structure to visual execution, motion, and front-end delivery. I value clarity, scalability, and intention in both design decisions and collaboration.
